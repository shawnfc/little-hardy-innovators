Welcome

Welcome to The Little Hardy Innovators Inc., where your child/children will embark on their journey to success. The development of our Day Care is to ensure families that we provide a safe, structured and cohesive learning environment for your child. With the knowledge that each child has an individual learning style, our program was created to develop social skills along with educational and innovative growth simultaneously.

Our Goal

To teach, expose, and inspire all children enrolled in our center that all things are possible, and cultivate their ideas into attainable goals. We think outside the box and no idea is foolish.

We believe in starting young, for their little minds are like sponges and this is a critical time frame to prepare them for their future life endeavors.
