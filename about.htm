About

In 2008 two sets of siblings, Tizje’ and Kahia Little along with Tyree and Sakai Hardy (ranging from ages 6-10) baked and sold cookies naming their company “The Little Hardy’s.” As their parents, we recognized their entrepreneurship, so we assisted them by supporting their creative ideas.

Our Goals

To teach, expose and inspire all children enrolled in our center that all things are possible, and cultivate their ideas into attainable goals. We realize that encouraging while guiding both children and youth’s thoughts of how they view themselves in their future is important.

The Little Hardy Innovators where
“The Future Creators are Children Innovators”
is practiced daily.

In a society where most of the population is consumer based and the same thoughts are merely passed around, we strive to be creators that pave the way for women presidents, musicians, publicists, authors, chefs, veterinarians.
