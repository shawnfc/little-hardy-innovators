Our Program

Our program starts from 6 weeks to 12 years and we are New York State licensed accepting private payments and HRA/ACD vouchers. We believe in the socialization and the educational well-being of each and every child, fully aware that children learn differently. Some are visual hands on learners, and some are text book learners so our program is designed to meet their individual needs.

Our daycare center does daily health checks in the morning when each child arrives, which entails a physical observation of our children for illnesses, bruising, and/or rashes that may be apparent.
